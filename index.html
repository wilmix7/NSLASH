Introduction:

NSLASH is a multilanguage support FRAMEWORK TECHNOLOGY and it is mainly used with JDOLLAR. NSLASH Technology is invented in JDollar in April 2015 by Wilmix Jemin J. NSLASH is the 12 th Most Advanced technology with VVMC chained Framework. NSLASH Uses WilmixcatServer to run .Nslash Programs. NSlash with JDOLLAR oops , .Nslash with Model and Controller Programs. .Nslash uses VVMC->VVMC/... (View with Model/view/Controller with View with Model/view/Controller ....). with Chaining support and it also generates .Class file. So NSLASH is a VVMC/ Chained Framework technology.

(It is part of JDollar part3)
